WORD OF THE YEAR

A Harvest of Many Seasons

"Behold, I have sent you to reap that for which you have not laboured; others have laboured, and you have entered into their labours."
John 4:38

This season, by the Word of the Lord, is a season of abundance. In Leviticus 25, God tells Israel, that He has commanded the blessing of the sixth year upon them, and that they would harvest for 3 years, while having not sowed. This is the season the church, and the continent of Africa has come into. Announce it over your life.
