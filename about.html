Who We Are

A Bible-Believing Church

We believe in Jesus Christ, and in the authority of His Word. The Word Revealed , the Present Word , and the Word Yet to Be Revealed .

Our desire is for people to know and love Jesus, for lives to be transformed by the Word of God, and for people to grow in their relationship with Jesus, serving Him and representing Him in every sphere of life.
